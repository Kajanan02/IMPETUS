This year's IMPETUS 2022 Article Writing Competition will be held as an inter-university competition, giving talented students from other universities a chance to compete. Valuable cash prizes will also be awarded to the winners of the competition. The article writing competition has given the tech enthusiasts with hidden talents a great opportunity to showcase their talents to the world. The winners of the IMPETUS 2022 article writing competition will also be featured in the artistic IMPETUS annual publication magazine.

Technical Article Competition

Best Skills

Your articles must be in English language.

Articles should have at least 500 words. Articles can be submitted in either PDF or Docx format and have to rename the document as IMPETUS2022_TW_YOURNAME.pdf or IMPETUS2022_TW_YOURNAME.docx

EX: - IMPETUS2022_TW_NirashaRathnayake.pdf or IMPETUS2022_TW_ NirashaRathnayake.docx

If you are use figures or images in your article, please put into another folder and create a zip file. The content must be original work by the contestant (plagiarism will not be entertained). Articles will be evaluated by distinguished evaluators with industrial and academic experience and will be assessed based on the following criteria

  • Content Clarity
  • Structure and The Flow
  • Grammar and Mechanics
  • Originality and Relevance to The Topic
